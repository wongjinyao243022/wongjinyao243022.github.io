Wong Jin Yao

"Surrender to no one. Only you can save yourself."


My name is Wong Jin Yao. I only submit to people I truly admire. I am interested in metaphysics because nothing and no one in that realm is bound by limits; everything holds infinite possibilities. Besides metaphysics, I also love technology. The reason is the same as my own personality: it offers endless potential and refuses to be restricted. The deeper you go, the more new things you discover.I dislike showing my weakness to others. Dog-eat-dog is the law of survival in this world, and extraordinary times call for extraordinary measures.

I have always been searching for the meaning of life and the definition of success. For the current me, success meant being able to do what I love without any restrictions. However, I later realized this definition of success is a bit unrealistic. Everything in this world has a dual nature. It is impossible for humans to be completely free from limits forever; everything is about balance. Now, I feel the meaning of my life is to break the limitations and rules that bind me, and to never stop improving myself. I think as a human, we must live with a sense of honor and loyalty, and must always able to face ourselves, our parents and the universe. This is me.
