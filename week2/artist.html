Andy On

Andy On (安志杰) is a Hong Kong actor, martial artist, and model who is well known for his action-packed performances in Asian cinema. Born on May 11, 1977, in the United States, he developed an interest in martial arts at a young age and trained extensively in disciplines such as taekwondo and boxing. Andy began his acting career after moving to Hong Kong and quickly gained attention for his athletic ability and screen presence.

He made his film debut in the movie Black Mask 2: City of Masks, where he took over a role originally associated with Jet Li. Over the years, Andy has appeared in numerous action films, including New Police Story, Invisible Target, and Special ID. His strong martial arts skills and dedication to performing challenging action scenes have earned him recognition as one of Hong Kong cinema's leading action stars. Besides acting, Andy is admired for his fitness, discipline, and commitment to inspiring others through hard work and perseverance.
